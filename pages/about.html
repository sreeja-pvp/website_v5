B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science)

A comprehensive four-year undergraduate program

Vision

To be a centre of excellence in academics and research in Computer Science and Engineering and take up challenges for the benefit of society.

Mission

  • Impart professional education through best curriculum in harmony with the industry needs.
  • Inculcate ethics, research capabilities and team work in the young minds so as to put efforts to the advancement of the nation.
  • Strive for student achievement and success with leadership qualities and preparing them for continuous learning in the global environment.

About the Program

B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) combines traditional computer science principles with data science techniques. The program covers a range of topics including data structures, algorithms, programming languages, operating systems, computer networks, and Data Science topics such as statistical analysis, data visualization, big data technologies, and machine learning.

Practical Learning Opportunities

The program emphasizes experiential learning through lab work, internships, capstone projects, and research. Students gain practical experience working with real-world datasets and utilizing advanced data science tools and techniques.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers in various industries, including technology, finance, healthcare, retail, and more. Roles include:

  • Data Scientist
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Business Intelligence Analyst
  • Data Engineer
  • Software Developer

Graduates can also opt for postgraduate studies in Data Science, AI, ML, or related fields.

Industries in Demand

Data scientists are in high demand across various industries, including:

  • Technology: Companies like Facebook, Apple, Amazon, Netflix, and Google use data science for algorithms, advertising systems, and recommendation engines.
  • Finance: Banks and insurance companies use data science for fraud detection and customer behavior analysis.
  • Healthcare: Data science is applied in precision medicine, drug development, and medical imaging.
  • Retail: Retailers use data for demand forecasting, inventory management, and pricing optimization.
  • Manufacturing: Data science helps with predictive maintenance and supply chain optimization.
  • Energy: Applied in power generation and distribution optimization.
  • Consulting: Consulting firms help businesses leverage data to enhance operations.
  • Government: Governments use data science for policymaking and budget allocation.
  • Cybersecurity: Protecting against cyber-attacks and data breaches.
  • Media and Advertising: Understanding customer preferences and optimizing ad placements.
